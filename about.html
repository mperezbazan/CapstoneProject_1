History

La Rienda is a band born in the city of Mendoza, Argentina in 2009. It's a group of young musicians who propose a particular style having traditional dances and Argentine folk music as a common language.
This group has toured numerous stages throughout Argentina, winners of the Diario UNO Stage Awards for the best traditional folk group, recognized by the Honorable Chamber of Senators of the province of Mendoza for their contribution to regional culture.
In his repertoire sounds and melodies are combined with the authenticity of modern instruments
